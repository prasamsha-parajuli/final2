Thrifting through Thriftstore

Thriftnest takes a sustainable approach to fashion by encouraging sellers to declutter their wardrobes and list a variety of items in our store. Our platform provides sellers with a way to earn money from pre-loved items, while guaranteeing buyers a curated selection of high-quality products. Buyers can enjoy a wide range of stylish options to suit their unique tastes without spending a large sum. With user-friendly features like organized categories, responsive design, and flexible pricing, Thriftnest creates an enjoyable thrifting experience for both sellers and buyers. Discover a sustainable fashion journey through Thriftnest today!

How to become a Seller?

Becoming a seller with Thriftnest means joining our community, and we're here to make the process as simple as possible.

  • Sign Up: Create an account to become a seller. Once registered, you'll have access to a personal dashboard where you can track your sales, adjust pricing, and manage delivery information.
  • Set Delivery Details:Provide delivery information for items you wish to thrift. We offer two options for item drop-off: either schedule a pickup or deliver them to our warehouse.
  • Inspection and Upload: Our team will inspect your items and decide what will be listed on our website. Items selected for thrifting will be uploaded, while others can be returned to you or donated.
  • Price Revisions: While you can adjust item pricing, we encourage trusting our pricing recommendations for optimized sales.
  • Sales Reports and Earnings: Access reports on your dashboard to view earnings from sold items. You'll receive 100% of the profit from your sales, making it a valuable opportunity to earn while supporting sustainable fashion.

It's a straightforward way to clear out your wardrobe, contribute to sustainability, and make extra cash with Thriftnest.

Thrift for Sustainability

Triftnest champions sustainability by empowering our customers to make conscious choices with pre-loved items at affordable prices. By opting for second-hand goods, our community helps to reduce clothing overconsumption, extending the life cycle of each garment and minimizing waste. This approach not only offers unique, high-quality items to our customers but also promotes an eco-conscious lifestyle, aiming to decrease the environmental footprint of the fashion industry.
